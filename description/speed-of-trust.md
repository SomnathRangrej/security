**The Speed of Trust** is a popular leadership and personal development book by Stephen M. R. Covey. It explains how **trust is not just a moral value—but a measurable economic driver** that affects speed and cost in organizations and relationships.

---

## 🚀 Core Idea

> **When trust goes up → speed increases & cost decreases**
> **When trust goes down → speed decreases & cost increases**

Example:

* High trust → quick decisions, less verification, faster execution
* Low trust → delays, approvals, audits, rework → higher cost

---

## 🔑 The 5 Waves of Trust

Covey describes trust as something that grows in layers:

1. **Self Trust**

   * Confidence in your own integrity and ability
   * Built through credibility (character + competence)

2. **Relationship Trust**

   * Trust between individuals
   * Built via consistent behavior and communication

3. **Organizational Trust**

   * Culture of trust within a company
   * Leads to better collaboration and innovation

4. **Market Trust**

   * Reputation and brand trust externally

5. **Societal Trust**

   * Contribution to society and public confidence

---

## ⚙️ The 4 Cores of Credibility

These define *self-trust*:

* **Integrity** → Are you honest?
* **Intent** → Why are you doing this?
* **Capabilities** → Do you have the skills?
* **Results** → Do you deliver outcomes?
